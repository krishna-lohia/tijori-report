Capex Watch

Grasim Industries committed ₹3,094 crore for Phase II Lyocell capacity, adding 110,000 tonnes per annum by 2030 and targeting 35% specialty fiber mix versus current 25-28%. Incremental revenue potential is ₹1,200 to ₹1,400 crore annually post-2030 with estimated ROCE of 18-20% and a 3.5 to 4 year payback. Disa India and Shanti Gold International both commenced commercial production at new facilities today. Kovil Lakshmi Roller approved windmill replacement capex. Eighty-seven capex facilities have now commissioned in five weeks, all racing to prove utilization and manage margin pressure in Q1 and Q2 FY2027 filings.
