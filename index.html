Capex Watch

Apollo Pipes and Shyam Metalics both went live today. Apollo started commercial production at its Mirzapur plant near Varanasi. Shyam Metalics commissioned a 0.15 MTPA colour-coated steel expansion, targeting ₹75 to 150 crore in incremental revenue with 18 to 22% margins after a year of ramp-up pressure. Waaree Energies began production at its Samakhiali solar subsidiary in Kutch. Welspun Living partially started pillow manufacturing in Nevada. Watch Q1 utilization rates and margin progression closely.
