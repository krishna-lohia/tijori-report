Capex Watch

Shyam Metalics announced ₹2,700 crore capex for SBQ expansion and a stainless steel unit, targeting 15 to 25% revenue growth by FY29 with EBITDA margin compression to 10 to 11% in FY27 to FY28. SRF approved capex, Bigbloc Construction filed an intimation, EPack Prefab and Hindustan Foods both announced capex commencements. Over sixty facilities have now commissioned in four weeks, with Bigbloc's Umargaon facility, Hind Organic, Balrampur, Subam, Apollo Pipes, and Shyam's earlier plants all racing to prove utilization in next quarter's numbers.
