Capex Watch

Five companies commenced commercial production today. Disa India started a ₹56 crore Tumkur facility targeting 15 to 25% revenue growth post-ramp. Eveready began alkaline battery manufacturing in Jammu. Chemplast Sanmar and Century Plyboards both brought new units online. These join over sixty-five facilities commissioned in the past four weeks. All face margin compression in FY2027 before operating leverage kicks in. Watch Q1 and Q2 FY2027 filings for utilization rates and EBITDA headwinds across this cohort.
