Capex Watch

Five more capex facilities went live today. Disa India commissioned a Tumkur plant, Eveready started alkaline battery production in Jammu, Chemplast Sanmar brought new capacity online, Shree Rama Multi-Tech added 45 lakh tubes per month targeting Q2 FY2027 commercialization, and Kovilpatti Lakshmi approved windmill repowering. Over eighty capex facilities have now commissioned in five weeks. All need to prove utilization and defend margins in Q1 and Q2 FY2027 filings. Track Disa, Eveready, Chemplast, Century Plyboards, Shyam Metalics, Apollo Pipes, Balrampur Chini, Subam Papers, EPack, Bigbloc, SRF, and Hindustan Foods closely.
