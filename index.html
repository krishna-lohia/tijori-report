Capex Watch

Sanjiv Shah is now on day four of buying and the capex wave keeps rolling. EPack Prefab commenced production at its Mambattu plant, a 19.4% capacity expansion that will pressure margins 100 to 150 basis points during ramp-up but should drive 18% to 22% EBITDA growth once utilization hits 75% to 80%. Hindustan Foods, Hind Organic Chemicals, and Balrampur Chini all filed capex updates. Balrampur revised its polylactic acid project spend. The commissioning count is now well past forty facilities in the past month. Hind Organic, Balrampur, EPack, Subam, Shyam, and Apollo are all racing to prove utilization before next quarter's filings.
