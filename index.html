Market Snapshot

Nifty 50 closed up 0.05% at barely positive territory. Next 50 rose 0.10%, midcaps fell 0.09%, smallcaps dropped 0.36%. Breadth was mixed, with large caps holding ground while smaller names gave back some of last week's gains. VIX spiked 9.18% intraday, the first meaningful tension in a month that had otherwise been compressing volatility. Still down 16% over 30 days, but today's jump is worth noting.

Capex Watch

Four new plants went live. Subam Papers started a 71% capacity expansion targeting high-margin paper grades, expecting near-term margin pressure from depreciation until a cogeneration unit starts in June. Apollo Pipes opened a Mirzapur facility. Shyam Metalics commenced production at a new unit. Waaree Energies' subsidiary, Sangam Solar One, started operations in Kutch. Trishikti Industries filed a capex update with no detail. Capacity is coming online across metals, pipes, paper, and solar at a steady clip.
