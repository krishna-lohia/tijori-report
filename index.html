Market Snapshot

Nifty 50 fell 0.36% to close the day in the red while Next 50 rose 0.49%, midcaps added 0.11%, and smallcaps gained 0.19%. The index is now down 1.1% year-on-year while smaller caps are up between 8.5% and 11.2% over the same period. VIX dropped another 2.13% and is now down 36.2% over the past month. Breadth was positive outside the large-cap index.
