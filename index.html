Capex Watch

Pitti Engineering approved ₹290 crore capex to double casting capacity from 18,600 to 36,000 metric tonnes by year three, targeting automotive and industrial demand. Purple Wave Infocom filed a press release about capex funding for a subsidiary. Shyam Metalics disclosed board meeting outcomes but provided no fresh capex detail beyond its ₹2,700 crore commitment already in memory. Bigbloc Construction filed an intimation, likely related to its Umargaon facility now ramping. SRF filed a revision in capex, no quantum disclosed. This brings the running total to over sixty-five commissioned or approved facilities in four weeks. Watch Q1 FY2027 filings for utilization and margin compression across Pitti, Shyam, Apollo, Balrampur, Subam, EPack, Bigbloc, and SRF.
